Database Design & Development

For this module students undertake a system investigation before creating their own software solution. The module is split into the following modules that provide students with the skills to become an effective database designer:

  1. Students prepare a systems investigation document and analyse the functional requriements of a software solution.
  2. Students develop their own solution based on the results of the system investigation document.
  3. Students evaluate their own work and reflect on what they developed.
